## 🏛️ About Us

**SKT AI LABS** is a frontier AI research and development organization headquartered in the heart of India. Founded and led by **Shrijan Kumar Tiwari**, we are a team of researchers, engineers, and data scientists committed to building **sovereign artificial intelligence** that serves the 1.4 billion people of Bharat.

> **Founded in:** Sidhi, Madhya Pradesh & Raipur, Chhattisgarh, India

### Our Philosophy

We believe that the future of AI must be **inclusive, culturally grounded, and sovereign**. While global AI models have made remarkable strides, they often fall short when it comes to understanding the linguistic diversity, cultural nuances, and practical needs of India. SKT AI LABS exists to bridge this gap.

### Why Sovereign AI Matters

| Challenge | Our Solution |
|-----------|-------------|
| 🔒 **Data Privacy** | Indian data stays within Indian infrastructure |
| 🗣️ **Language Barriers** | Deep mastery of Hindi, Hinglish, and regional dialects |
| 🔗 **API Dependency** | Reduced reliance on foreign model providers |
| 🌍 **Cultural Context** | Models aligned with Indian values and societal context |
| ⚡ **Latency & Cost** | Optimized for Indian hardware and network realities |

## 🎯 Mission & Vision

### Mission
> To develop world-class, sovereign AI systems that understand, respect, and serve the linguistic and cultural diversity of India, while maintaining the highest standards of data privacy and technological independence.

### Vision
> A future where every Indian developer, startup, and enterprise has access to frontier AI that speaks their language, understands their context, and operates within their control — without dependency on foreign technology.

## ⚙️ Technical Specifications

### Model Architecture

```yaml
Organization:      SKT AI LABS
Model Series:      SKT Surya Series
Architecture:      Custom Transformer-based (SKT_ST-X-0)
Specialization:    Advanced Coding, Logical Reasoning, Hinglish Mastery
Quantization:      GGUF, EXL2, AWQ (Broad Accessibility)
```

### Training Infrastructure

| Component | Specification |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Compute** | 16× NVIDIA A100 GPU Nodes |
| **Storage** | 8 EB (Exabyte) Cluster |
| **Framework** | Custom optimized training pipeline |

### Training Data

| Dataset | Size | Description |
|---------|------|-------------|
| **Indic Corpus** | 16T+ tokens | Cleaned Hindi/Indic language tokens |
| **Magnum Corpus** | 10.26 TB | High-entropy diverse dataset |
| **Hinglish Dataset** | 10M+ samples | High-quality Hinglish with deep reasoning chains |
| **Vedic/Sanskrit** | Private | Specialized cultural and historical corpus |
| **Coding Examples** | Extensive | Programming and logical reasoning data |

### Performance Targets

- ✅ **Zero Hallucination** — Fact-grounded responses
- ✅ **110+ tok/s** — Real-time inference capability
- ✅ **Deep Hinglish** — Slang, idioms, and dialect mastery
- ✅ **Multi-Modal** — Text, image, audio, and video understanding
- ✅ **Energy Efficient** — Optimized for accessible hardware

## 🤝 Contributing

We welcome contributions from researchers, engineers, data contributors, and AI enthusiasts who share our vision of sovereign AI for India.

### How to Contribute

1. **🍴 Fork** the repository
2. **🌿 Create** a feature branch (`git checkout -b feature/amazing-feature`)
3. **💾 Commit** your changes (`git commit -m 'Add amazing feature'`)
4. **📤 Push** to the branch (`git push origin feature/amazing-feature`)
5. **🔀 Open** a Pull Request

### Contribution Areas
